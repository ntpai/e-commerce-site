Name: OV7670 Camera Module (Arduino/STM)

Description:
The OV7670 camera module is a low-cost, 0.3-megapixel (640x480) VGA CMOS camera with a Digital Signal Processor (DSP) that can be controlled via a Serial Camera Control Bus (SCCB) interface. It is commonly used in embedded and Arduino projects for its ability to capture both images and video at up to 30 frames per second (fps). The module offers various image processing functions, such as white balance, color saturation, and gamma control, which can be programmed to suit different applications. 

Price: rs 250
